Purpose

I believe I can use my imagination and skills to improve the quality of life of others. With that in mind, I aim to build things that are useful, helpful, enjoyable, and thought-provoking.

I want to help others recognize their endless capacity for creative thinking. By thinking, speaking, and living creatively I hope to show that there are no set rules for how they should think or approach their work, business, career, and life.

I’m also figuring all this out as I go. I’ll surely update the contents of this page as time goes on.
